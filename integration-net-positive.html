Integration Net Positive (Affirmative)

Intro Round

Integration is a net positive for Black Americans because it expanded legal rights, economic access, and political representation at scale.

Premise Round

  • Legal rights: Civil-rights protections created enforceable access.
  • Economic access: More entry to schools, professions, capital networks.
  • Political power: Broader vote access and public office influence.

Rebuttal Round

  • “Integration harmed Black institutions” → Some losses are real, but net gains in rights/opportunity are larger.
  • “Outcomes still unequal” → That argues enforcement gaps, not that integration is net negative.

Rapid Fire

  • Do you prefer legal segregation over legal equality?
  • Are you rejecting integration or policy failures after integration?
  • Would you trade voting/civil-rights gains away?
