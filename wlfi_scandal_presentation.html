SEGMENT 01

What is WLFI?

The Structure

World Liberty Financial is structured so that DT Marks DEFI LLC — the Trump family's designated revenue vehicle — receives 75% of net revenues, while the legal wrapper specifically protects the Trump family from operational liability.[36]

The Extraction

The Trump family has extracted at least $890 million in revenues from WLFI while holding tokens valued at approximately $3.8 billion, with no documented personal capital investment at inception.[31]

The Founders

Co-founders include Eric Trump, Donald Trump Jr., alongside partners Zachary Folkman, Chase Herro, and Zach Witkoff.[51]

The Bank Charter Gambit

On January 9, 2026, WLFI applied to the OCC for a national trust bank charter under World Liberty Trust Company, with Zach Witkoff listed as proposed president — meaning they want to become a federally regulated bank.[43]

SEGMENT 02

The Blacklist Bombshell

The core accusation: a hidden kill switch embedded in the smart contract, allowing the seizure of any investor's assets without notice or vote.

The August 2025 Upgrade

World Liberty changed the smart contract governing $WLFI to add a "blacklisting" function — without governance vote or disclosure.

"While the upgrade is technically visible on the public blockchain, World Liberty buried it in the code without alerting token holders to its existence or implications. In the dark of night, the company thus created a 'blacklisting' function that it could wield at will."

— FEDERAL COMPLAINT, APRIL 21, 2026[2]

The modification was not put to a governance vote or disclosed to investors, even as token holders had just approved a proposal to make a portion of the supply tradable.[2]

World Liberty's own risk disclosure documents explicitly state the company can block and freeze wallet addresses if it deems them linked to activities that violate project terms.

The Multisig

Control Structure 3-of-5
Guardian Wallet Anonymous
Freeze Power 1 Signature
Seizure Power 3 Signatures

One person — one single individual — has the unilateral power to freeze any token holder's assets. Seizing those assets requires a 3-of-5 multisig vote, but freezing requires only one signature.[35]

The Admission

They admitted it in the fine print. The risk disclosure explicitly states the company can block and freeze wallet addresses and associated tokens.

This is not DeFi. This is a centralized bank with no regulations.
